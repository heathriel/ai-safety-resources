# AI Safety Case Studies

Real-world incidents where AI systems caused harm, violated principles, or exposed systemic risks. Each case study analyzes what went wrong, which ethical principles were violated, and the lessons learned.

> These cases are documented for educational purposes to help organizations anticipate and prevent similar failures.

## Case Studies

| Case | Year | Domain | Primary Failure | Principle Violated |
|------|------|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| [Amazon Recruiting Tool](amazon-recruiting-tool.md) | 2014-2017 | HR / Hiring | Gender bias in resume screening | Fairness, Accountability |
| [Microsoft Tay](microsoft-tay.md) | 2016 | Social Media | Adversarial prompt injection (jailbreaking) | Robustness, Safety |
| [Air Canada Chatbot](air-canada-chatbot.md) | 2022-2024 | Customer Service | Hallucination leading to legal liability | Reliability, Accountability |
| [Clearview AI](clearview-ai.md) | 2020-2022 | Surveillance | Unauthorized data scraping, privacy violations | Privacy, Consent, Accountability |
| [COMPAS Recidivism](compas-recidivism.md) | 2016 | Criminal Justice | Racial bias in risk assessment | Fairness, Transparency |
| [GPT-3 Medical Misinformation](gpt3-medical.md) | 2020 | Healthcare | Hallucination of dangerous medical advice | Reliability, Safety |

## How to Use These Case Studies

- **Training**: Use as discussion prompts for AI ethics training sessions
- **Risk Assessment**: Reference during project planning to anticipate similar failure modes
- **Incident Response**: Adapt lessons learned into your organization's incident response playbook
